    • “Education is the great engine of personal development. It is through education that the daughter of a peasant can become a doctor, that the son of a mineworker can become the head of the mine, that a child of farm workers can become the president of a great nation. It is what we make out of what we have, not what we are given, that separates one person from another.” -Nelson Mandela Importance Of Education in Children’s Life Children are the future of a nation. They learn what is taught to them through education and build their personality. Education is an important part of children’s lives. It improves their skill, personality, and attitude. “Education is the foundation upon which we build our future.” Education brings self-discipline, a sense of responsibility, teamwork to children and prevents them from feeling social insecurity. It helps in being self-confident and a good decision-maker.

    Importance Of Education in Society Education is a tool that helps an individual know their rights and duties. Education in society provides knowledge, jobs, skills, and cultural norms values. An educated society nurtures the younger generation providing them with the needed facilities and training. Education is essential in a society as it helps individuals gain sufficient academic qualifications and enables them to get suitable employment. A society that values education flourishes in its social, economic, and financial sectors.
